Recipe #1

  • Time: 30
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients Quantity
Rice Krispies 6 cups
Marshmallows 40
Butter 3 tablespoons

Step 1: Melt butter in pan
Step 2: Add marshallows and stir
Step 3: Add rice krispie cereal

Source: The Original Rice Krispie Recipe

Recipe #2

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Rootbeer 2 quarts
Vanilla bean ice cream 1 pint

Step 1: Add a big scoop of ice cream in glass
Step 2: Fill glass 3/4 full with rootbeer
Step 3: Enjoy!

Source: Rootbeer Floats by Rachael Ray

Recipe #3

  • Time: 13 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 2 teaspoons
Bread 2 slices
Peanut butter 1 teaspoon
Jelly (any flavor) 2 teaspoons

Step 1: Heat butter in skillet
Step 2: Spread butter on each slice of bread
Step 3: Spread peanut butter on one slice and jelly on the other
Step 4: Grill each side for 4 minutes and enjoy!

Source: Grilled Peanut butter & jelly sandwich
